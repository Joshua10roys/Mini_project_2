About

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931, who was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu

He studied physics and aerospace engineering in Madras Institute of Technology in 1960

He spent the four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)

Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile.

He served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007

In 26th May 2005, Switzerland has declared May 26 as the Science Day in Honour of President APJ Abdul Kalam visit.
